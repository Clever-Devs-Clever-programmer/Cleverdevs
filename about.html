About the Game

This is a quiz game that will test your knowledge of coding languages. You will be asked 10 questions and will be awarded points based on how many tries it takes to get the correct answer. If you select the incorrect answer, clicking the next button will show you the question again and give you the opportunity to try again. If you have clicked the correct answer, you will get points and clicking the next button will bring you to the next question.
